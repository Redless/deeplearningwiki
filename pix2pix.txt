====== pix2pix ======


From [[Berkeley AI Research]]: https://arxiv.org/pdf/1611.07004v1.pdf

A pixel in, pixel out [[GANs|GAN]] with incredibly strong results. They use a [[U-Net]] like architecture to acheive these results.
