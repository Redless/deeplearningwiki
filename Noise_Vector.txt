====== Noise Vector ======

A noise vector is a vector of random noise fed into the [[Generator]] of a [[GANs|GAN]], which will then produce an output approximating the [[Training Data]]. According to https://github.com/soumith/ganhacks, a gaussian distribution makes for the most effective noise vector.
