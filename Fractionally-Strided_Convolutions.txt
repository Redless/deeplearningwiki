====== Fractionally-Strided Convolutions ======
Created Monday 13 August 2018

https://arxiv.org/pdf/1609.05158.pdf from twitter.

Among other things. 

A convolutional layer that has a fractional stride. This results in upscaling, an output that's larger than its input.

The paper says that this is commonly implemented with "interpolation, perforate, or un-pooling".
